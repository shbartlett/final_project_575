History of Disasters in FEMA Region IV, 1950 - 2019

The US Federal Emergency Management Agency (FEMA) allocates and deploys resources and assistance to communities in need after major disasters.

FEMA declared disasters categories contain both natural and man-made events including flooding, fire, and hurricanes.

This visualization focuses on the history of events in FEMA Region IV, which is an area of responsibility encompassing the Southeastern United States.

Declared disaster data available from FEMA's open data initiative.

Project created by Dwight F., Stephanie B., & Moe R. Ⓒ 2019
